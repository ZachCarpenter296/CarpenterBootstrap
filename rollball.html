Roll-a-Ball

The Roll-a-Ball project is a simple game we designed for the use of a controller me and some classmates made. We needed a proof of concept to show our controller, which was a joystick on an arduino board, could work. The objective of the game was to collect all the yellow cubes as quickly as possible, as the second you moved the ball, you were getting timed. We even held a small competition to see who could collect them the fastest, and the winning time was just over 4 seconds.
